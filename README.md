# MiliraTechGate
A small patch for mod Milira Race

The main problem with the Milira race mod was that they appearing too early, especially if your mod pack is designed around the gradual progression of a colony through the technological eras. If you have Combat Extended installed, it’s practically impossible to fight these angels because of their deadly blasters and heavy armor until the late Industrial era, when you have armor-piercing weapons and EMP grenades and ammo. Think of them as “prettier mechanoids.”

## What does this mod do?

* Intercepts Milira Race events, preventing them from happening until the specified tech era is reached (Medieval, Industrial, Spacer, Ultra, Archotech).
* This patch intercepts the triggering of Milira Race events, such as:
  - IncidentWorker_Milian_SmallCluster_SingleTurret 
  - IncidentWorker_MiliraCluster
  - IncidentWorker_RaidMilira

## Dependencies:
- Harmony
- Milira Race mod

## Installation:
- Put "MiliraTechGate" folder into "Mods" folder in game directory.
- Place it lower Milira Race mod and any other addons and patches for it. Use RimSort or in-game mod menu.
